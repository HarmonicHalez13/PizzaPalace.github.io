About Us!

My name is Vincent Ramano and I am the owner of Pizza Palace. My family left Sicily when I was young, but that didn't stop my ma and pa from showing me the traditional ways of creating Italian food. I moved to Los Angeles from Brooklyn at the age of 20. I knew immediately that I wanted to show others the experience of authentic Italian food. With the help of my wife, we opened The Pizza Palace and now I have another generation taking over after me! Our family-owned restauraunt is sure to bring smiles to all who come and enjoy our cuisine!

We only specialize in Italy's finest pizza pies! We have special made brick stove ovens for the best Italian pizza in LA!
